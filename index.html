Our Story

Rooted in Schoharie
Since 1889

F.A. Guernsey & Co. has been a cornerstone of the Schoharie community for over 135 years. What started as a family passion for horticulture has grown into the region's most trusted full-service nursery and landscape design company.

Three generations of nursery expertise — we know what grows best in upstate New York's unique climate and soil.

A personal approach to landscape design — we listen to your vision and work with you every step of the way.
